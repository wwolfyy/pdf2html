text parsing strategy

- pymupdf xhtml
    detects superscripts (<sup> </sup>)
    reads texts by line (tagged as <p>) (paragraph if footnote)

- pymupdf dict
    doesn't detect superscripts
    reads texts by word (tagged as <span>)

- pymupdf json
    doesn't detect superscripts
    reads texts by line (tagged as <span>)

- naver OCR
    can't detect superscript
    reads texts by word
